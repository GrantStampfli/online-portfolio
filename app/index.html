About Me...

I'm a junior devoloper with a deep passion for designing and developing websites – desktop and mobile. I believe in cutting edge web technologies, and care for semantics and accessibility.

Design and back-end development are essential for the web, but the exciting area where they meet is front-end development. As a front-end developer, I get to take a design – something that’s beautiful but static – and turn it into an interface, where its components become tools for unique experiences on the web.

So much is at stake at this stage! The best design can be mangled in the hands of someone uninterested or unwilling to juggle the complexities of cross-browser differences. The best deployment can cause problems down the road if not built to be reusable and adaptable.

I'm not a specialist. Even better. Some people specialize in certain areas of design or development, I want to have one foot in each camp.

I want to be creative – both when working with user interfaces and code. I want to develop tools which have value for people.

I'm a team-player and am not afraid to build bridges or light fires. I dislike complexity, verbosity, and clunky detours.


A great front-end developer brings designs to the fullness of the designers intention and creates up a webpage to be flexible and successful for years into the future.
